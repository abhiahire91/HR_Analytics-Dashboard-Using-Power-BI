<h2 style="color:#9333ea;">📌 Project Overview</h2>

<div style="background:linear-gradient(135deg,#1f1c2c,#928dab); padding:15px; border-radius:12px; color:white;">
  This project presents a <b>Power BI HR Analytics Dashboard</b> designed to analyze workforce data and uncover key insights 
  related to <b>employee attrition, salary trends, job satisfaction, and demographics</b>.
</div>

<br>

<ul>
  <li>📊 Understand employee turnover patterns</li>
  <li>📉 Identify key factors driving attrition</li>
  <li>📈 Enable data-driven HR decisions</li>
</ul>

<h2 style="color:#9333ea;">🚀 Key Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>✔️ Employee Count & Attrition Overview</li>
  <li>✔️ Attrition Rate Analysis (16%)</li>
  <li>✔️ Salary Slab Distribution</li>
  <li>✔️ Attrition by Job Role</li>
  <li>✔️ Job Satisfaction Insights</li>
  <li>✔️ Attrition by Age & Experience</li>
  <li>✔️ Gender-based Attrition Analysis</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2 style="color:#9333ea;">📊 Dashboard Insights</h2>

<div style="background:#0f172a; padding:15px; border-radius:12px; color:white;">
  🔹 High attrition in <b>Life Sciences & Medical</b><br><br>
  🔹 Majority employees fall under <b>5K salary slab</b><br><br>
  🔹 <b>Sales & Laboratory roles</b> show higher attrition<br><br>
  🔹 Early career employees (0–5 yrs) more likely to leave
</div>
